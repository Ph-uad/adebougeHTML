What are you building and for whom?

Adebouge Holdings enables Education to Employment (E2E) opportunities for young talents in emerging economies and ensures competitive market returns for investors by facilitating incentivized stakeholder collaborations through public-private partnerships with local authorities, investors, and technology-enabled startups and businesses.

Our model focuses on identifying and exploring intrinsic incentives in stakeholders relationships and leverage technology infrastructure to integrate STEM-related Vocational Technical Education (VTE) in Senior Schools' curricula, addressing structural unemployment in emerging market communities in sub Saharan Africa, such as Surulere, a major local jurisdiction in Lagos State, Nigeria.
