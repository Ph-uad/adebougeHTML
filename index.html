adebouge holdings

Adebouge Holdings is a research-driven tech company that addresses youth unemployment in emerging economies through unique business as a service approach and enabling impact investing projects through collaboration that strategic U.S Nigeria partnerships.

We work with partners in the target market to reposition and revitalize local high potential businesses by enhancing operational capabilities and fostering profitability. Our aim is to transform small ventures into potential global multibillion-dollar organizations that create sustainable job opportunities for young people.

Bayo Adebogun

Research, Technology, jobs

Bayo Adebogun, cofounder of Adebouge Holdings was educated in Nigeria and the US, brings unique leadership experience from Nigerian education, government, and tech sectors. With a decade managing Public-Private Partnerships and 20+ years in global enterprise project management, Bayo, a certified PMP, co-founded multiple companies. Actively engaged with University of Chicago Booth entrepreneurship resources, he participates in events by the Polsky Center, Rustandy Center, and Alumni series. Bayo's philanthropic efforts include educational funding through Adebouge Holdings collaboration, mentorship for the University of Kansas, and advocacy on economic matters in emerging markets and technologies with Eldridge Investments Group. He enjoys outdoor activities with his family.
