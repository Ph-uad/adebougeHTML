adebouge holdings

Adebouge Holdings is a research-driven tech company which addresses youth unemployment in emerging economies through unique business as a service approach and enabling impact investing projects through collaboration with strategic U.S.-Nigeria partnerships.

We work with partners in the target market to reposition and revitalize local high potential businesses by enhancing operational capabilities and foster profitability. Our aim is to transform small ventures into potential global multibillion-dollar organizations that create sustainable job opportunities for young people.
